What is WaterTrackr?

Watertrackr is a smart device created to track your water usage.



How does it work?

The device is attached to your pipe and it uses vibrations to measure the amount of water used daily. The data is then sent to users through our app.


Where do I Sign Up?

Use the form on our Contact Us page to get notified of our official launch. After submitting your information, you will receive an email with the information required to get started with us.


Become a Beta Tester

Once you have signed up to be notified, you will also receive an email inviting you to beta test our product. Once you are approved for our beta testing, you will receive a limited amount of custom Watertrackr smart devices to attach to the pipes in your house. In the first month of testing, you will be able to see:

  1. 1.Where water is used the most (Kitchen, Bathroom, Laundry Room,etc)
  2. 2.If you have any leaks
  3. 3.If water is left running


By the second month,you'll have the abiliy to set a water budget and our app will help you stay on target.
